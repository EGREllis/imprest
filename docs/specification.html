Specification

I am torn between making a mobile app or a website. It seems to me that it would be better as a mobile app (authentication and integration with the camera of a smartphone for photoing receipts seems convienient, and we need the appication to be easy to use).

In the interest of expediency the first draft will be a website even though it really ought to be a mobile app.

User stories

  1. As a staff member I am able to log in securely using my credentials.
  2. As a staff member, once I log in I am prompted the select the location of my shift.
  3. As a staff member, once my location has been determined, I see a summary of the list of room numbers and the available funds of the account of each room.
  4. As a staff member, I can click on a room number and see a summary of the activity of the account, including cash issued, time and date and by which staff member, cash recieved time and date and by which staff member.
  5. As a staff member, I have the option to issue cash to room numbers that have available funds.
  6. As a staff member, I do not have the option of issuing cash to room numbers that either do not have funds, or have unaccounted cash already issued.
  7. As a staff member, I have the option of accounting for outstanding cash issued to any room number.
  8. As a staff member, when I account for cash issued to room numbers, I am required to upload a photo of a receipt, and write an amount for which the account should be debited. Alternatively, I must record that no receipt was provided.
  9. As a staff member, when accounting for cash issued I should be able to write a note, readable by other staff members, when accounting for cash.
  10. As a staff member, I should be able to request an audit of a location. This audit should detail every transaction by every room number, including photos uploaded of the reciepts and the who issued the cash and who accounted for any change returned.
